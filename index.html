Let's Work For the Environment

Hack The Earth, is a hackathon that is themed around the environment where participants aim to invent sustainable and green technology for the betterment of our planet. It is an environmental initiative with the goal to promote sustainable technology in an ever-changing, adapting industry, where technological waste is commonplace.
